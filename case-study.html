Case Study: Fortune 500 Wire Fraud Recovery

A comprehensive breakdown of our successful $4.2M wire fraud recovery for a leading manufacturing corporation.

The Challenge

A multinational manufacturing company's finance department received what appeared to be a legitimate wire transfer request from their CEO. The email contained company letterhead, proper formatting, and specific project details. Within minutes, $4.2 million was transferred to an international account. The fraud was only discovered during the end-of-day reconciliation.

Lessons Learned

  • Speed Matters: Acting within the first 24 hours increased recovery rate by 67% compared to delayed responses
  • Multi-Channel Coordination: Simultaneous work with banks, law enforcement, and legal teams is essential
  • Documentation: Preserved all email headers, IP logs, and transaction records for ongoing investigation
  • Preventative Measures: Implemented enhanced verification protocols for large wire transfers
