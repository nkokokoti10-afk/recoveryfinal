Stablecoin Recovery

Specialized recovery strategies for USDC, USDT, DAI, and other stablecoin assets. Navigate frozen accounts and exchange holds with our expert team.

Understanding Stablecoins

How Stablecoin Recovery Works

Stablecoins are pegged to real-world assets like USD but operate across multiple blockchains and platforms. This complexity requires forensic analysis of smart contracts, exchange liquidity, and bridge protocols.

Multi-Chain Tracing

Stablecoins exist on Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, Avalanche, and more. We track balances across all chains and bridges.

Frozen Account Recovery

Stablecoins are subject to issuer freezes (USDC) and blacklisting. We work with issuers to unfreeze or migrate assets.

Bridge & Liquidity Analysis

Stablecoins move via bridges (Stargate, Across, Connext). We map liquidity flows and locked amounts.
