USDT Recovery

Tether (USDT) is one of the most widely used stablecoins across Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, and other blockchains. Our specialized USDT recovery service focuses on tracing assets, coordinating with exchanges, and working with Tether's compliance team to recover frozen or misrouted tokens.

What We Handle

Exchange-Held USDT

When USDT is frozen or held on major exchanges (Binance, Kraken, Coinbase), we liaise directly with compliance teams to document and release your assets.

Phishing & Theft

Unauthorized transfers of USDT across chains. We trace the flow, identify custodial endpoints, and pursue recovery through legal and issuer channels.

Bridge Issues & Lost Tokens

USDT transfers gone wrong via bridges (Stargate, Connext)? We map the liquidity and coordinate recovery with protocol teams.

Tether Coordination

Direct relationships with Tether's compliance office enable faster verification, unfreeze requests, and asset recovery documentation.

Next Steps

Have a USDT recovery case? Contact us with your transaction details, wallet address, and timeline. We'll assess and provide a recovery plan within 24 hours.
