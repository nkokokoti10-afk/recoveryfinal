Frequently Asked Questions

How long does initial assessment take?

We aim to acknowledge all submissions within 2 hours and provide a preliminary assessment within 24 hours for most cases. Emergency cases receive priority and can be assessed within 15 minutes.

Do you work with law enforcement?

Yes. We coordinate with law enforcement agencies and legal partners in multiple jurisdictions when appropriate. We provide evidence packages for investigations and support prosecution efforts.

Is there a cost for initial assessment?

Initial case assessment and triage are complimentary. Fees apply for extended investigation, forensic analysis, legal work, and recovery coordination based on scope and case complexity.

How do you handle confidentiality?

We maintain strict client confidentiality under attorney-client privilege where applicable. We publish anonymized case studies only with explicit client consent.

What information do I need to provide?

Provide transaction IDs, wallet addresses, screenshots of conversations, incident timeline, and exchange names. Provide all information necessary for recovery.
