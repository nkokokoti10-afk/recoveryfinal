Factors Affecting Recovery Success

Positive Factors

  • Funds deposited to regulated exchange
  • Report within 24-72 hours
  • Complete evidence documentation
  • Relatively large transaction amount
  • Transaction on major blockchain (ETH, BTC)
  • KYC'd recipient account
  • Law enforcement cooperation

Negative Factors

  • Funds sent to perpetrator wallet
  • Delayed reporting (>2 weeks)
  • Minimal documentation available
  • Very small transaction amount
  • Mixer/privacy coins used
  • Funds exited to fiat (cash out)
  • International jurisdiction conflicts
