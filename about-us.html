Our Story

CyberProRecover was founded in 2018 by blockchain security engineers who saw a critical gap: stolen cryptocurrency was technically traceable, but recovery required coordination that most victims couldn't navigate alone.

What started as pro-bono work with exchange partners evolved into a specialized firm that now handles complex multi-jurisdictional cases. Our early success came from rapid account freezes and strategic legal action that prevented cash-outs during critical windows.

Today: CyberProRecover combines proprietary blockchain forensics, 50+ exchange partnerships, and legal expertise across 15 jurisdictions to deliver recovery solutions that work.

Company Timeline
