Bank Wire Reversal & Recovery

Expert intervention for fraudulent wire transfers. Time is critical—we act immediately to freeze funds before they're withdrawn.

Common Wire Fraud Tactics

CEO Fraud: Scammers impersonate executives requesting urgent wire transfers to fraudulent accounts.

Vendor Spoofing: Fake invoices mimicking legitimate suppliers redirect payments to attacker accounts.

Account Compromise: Hackers gain access to business accounts and authorize unauthorized wire transfers.

Phishing Attacks: Credentials stolen through phishing emails leading to unauthorized wire access.

⚡ TIME-CRITICAL ACTION

Recovery Window

First 24 hours: 78% recovery rate if immediate action taken
24-48 hours: 45% recovery rate as funds begin dispersal
After 48 hours: 12% recovery rate, funds likely already withdrawn

Every minute counts. Contact us immediately upon discovering fraudulent wire activity.

Wire Reversal Strategy

  • Immediate Bank Contact: Halt transactions and alert sending/receiving banks simultaneously
  • Beneficiary Bank Coordination: Request fund freeze at receiving institution
  • Law Enforcement Liaison: File reports with FBI IC3 and local authorities for legal support
  • SWIFT Network Alert: Coordinate with SWIFT for international wire blocks
  • Legal Intervention: Obtain court orders freezing beneficiary accounts

Recovery Success Rates

Quick Action (0-24hrs): 78% success rate for full fund recovery

Medium Timeline (24-48hrs): 45% success rate for partial recovery

Delayed Action (48hrs+): 12% success rate, funds usually dispersed
