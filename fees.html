Frequently Asked Questions

What if you don't recover any funds?

We don't charge anything. Period. Our fee is contingent on successful recovery. You only pay from the recovered amount.

What if only partial recovery is achieved?

Our fee applies only to the amount actually recovered and transferred to you. If we recover $50K of a $100K loss, we charge our percentage on the $50K only.

Are there any upfront costs?

Absolutely not. No consultation fees, no assessment fees, no retainers. The only cost is our percentage of recovered funds.

Do you cover legal fees?

In most cases, yes. Our partnerships with law firms mean we absorb legal costs as part of our service. For complex international cases, legal costs may be discussed separately.

How are recovered funds delivered?

Wire transfer to your designated bank account. We coordinate all exchanges and handle KYC requirements. Funds are typically delivered within 3-5 business days after recovery is finalized.

What about taxes on recovered funds?

We provide comprehensive documentation for tax purposes. Consult your tax advisor regarding deductibility of losses and treatment of recovered funds.

Can you recover funds from non-regulated exchanges?

Potentially, but with lower recovery probability. If funds are held at unregulated exchanges or in perpetrator wallets, we pursue civil asset recovery and criminal prosecution.

Is payment plan available?

For enterprise cases, we can negotiate alternative fee structures including hourly billing, retainers, or hybrid models.
