Credit Card Chargeback Recovery

Specialized chargeback assistance for fraudulent charges, unauthorized transactions, and merchant disputes on credit cards.

Why Chargebacks Matter

Credit card chargebacks are your strongest protection against fraud. We manage the entire process, maximizing your recovery rate through expert documentation and merchant dispute handling.

5-Step Chargeback Process

1

Initial Dispute Filing

File formal dispute with your credit card issuer within required timeframe with complete documentation.

2

Evidence Compilation

Gather transaction records, communications, receipts, and proof of fraud or non-delivery.

3

Bank Investigation

Bank investigates your claim and may contact merchant for response or documentation.

4

Merchant Response Review

Counter merchant arguments with additional evidence strengthening your position.

5

Final Determination

Receive chargeback decision and credit reinstatement from your bank.
