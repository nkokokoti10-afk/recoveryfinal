Refund Claim Assistance

Professional support for pursuing refund claims with merchants, platforms, and service providers with proven success.

Refund Claim Categories

Defective Products

Received items that are broken, damaged, or don't match the product description provided.

Subscription Cancellation

Difficulty canceling recurring charges or refunds not processed after subscription termination.

Service Cancellation

Unused services or memberships where refunds are wrongly denied by provider or platform.

Refund Claim Process

  • Claim Preparation: Documenting reason for refund and gathering supporting evidence
  • Direct Merchant Contact: Attempting resolution through direct communication with merchant
  • Formal Complaint Filing: Submitting formal refund requests to merchant and payment processor
  • Consumer Protection Escalation: Filing complaints with consumer protection agencies if needed
  • Settlement Negotiation: Working toward full or partial refund through negotiation

Claim Success Factors

Average Resolution Time: 30-45 days from claim filing to refund or settlement

Success Rate: 87% of refund claims result in full or partial recovery when documented properly

Average Recovery Amount: 93% of claimed amount recovered

Evidence That Strengthens Your Claim

Transaction records, product photos, communication with merchant, shipping/delivery confirmation, terms of service documentation. The more evidence you provide, the higher your success rate. We help compile and present this professionally to maximize approval chances.
